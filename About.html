About Us

THE BEST MMA CLUB IN DOAHA CITY

The Sbg Doha is a community of martial arts enthusiasts in Doha who are dedicated to practicing and promoting the sport of mixed martial arts (MMA). Our club was founded in 2010 by a group of MMA enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where they could train and compete in a safe and supportive environment. Since then, we have grown into a thriving community of fighters and trainers who are committed to helping each other improve their skills and achieve their goals in the sport. Our club offers a variety of training programs and classes, including Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Kick boxing, and cross fit. We also host regular sparring sessions, competitions, and workshops with guest instructors from around the world. Our facilities include a full-sized MMA cage, a boxing ring, and a wide range of training equipment.
